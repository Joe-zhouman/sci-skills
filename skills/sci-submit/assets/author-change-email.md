# 作者变更邮件模板

> 期刊通常要求：**每位作者书面同意** + **通讯作者的变更理由**。以下是发给期刊编辑的邮件模板，每人一份。填好 `[brackets]`，让每个人**用自己的邮箱发，抄送通讯作者**。
>
> **这种每人各自发同意声明的，是唯一的例外。** 除此之外，任何联系编辑的邮件（催稿、问状态、问系统问题）——必须由通讯作者邮箱发出。编辑只认通讯邮箱。

## 怎么用

你是一作/投稿操作人，实际发邮件的事不需要你来干。你把模板填好，发给对应的作者，让他们自己复制粘贴发出去。

1. 从下面选对应的模板，替每人填好 `[brackets]`
2. 把每份填好的邮件发给那个人——"你用自己的邮箱把这封发出去，抄送通讯"
3. 所有人发完之后，通讯发汇总那封

---

## 模板 1：未受影响的作者（只需同意）

```
Subject: Consent: author list change for "[Manuscript Short Title]"

Dear Editor,

I am a co-author of the manuscript entitled "[Full Manuscript Title]"
[ (MS# [number], submitted to [Journal Name]) ].

I confirm that I am aware of and agree with the proposed change to the
author list: [简述变更 — added [Name] / removed [Name] /
reordered [Name] from position X to position Y].

[Your Full Name]
[Affiliation]
[Email]

CC: [Corresponding Author Name] <[Corresponding Author Email]>
```

---

## 模板 2：被影响的作者（加了/删了/调了，同意 + 确认）

```
Subject: Consent: author list change for "[Manuscript Short Title]"

Dear Editor,

I am a co-author of the manuscript entitled "[Full Manuscript Title]"
[ (MS# [number], submitted to [Journal Name]) ].

I confirm that I am aware of and agree with the proposed change to the
author list: [选一个：
  — 加人：I have been added to the author list. I confirm that I meet
    the authorship criteria per ICMJE recommendations.
  — 删人：I have been removed from the author list. I confirm that I
    agreed to this removal.
  — 调序：My position has changed from X to Y. I confirm that I agreed
    to this change.]

[Your Full Name]
[Affiliation]
[Email]

CC: [Corresponding Author Name] <[Corresponding Author Email]>
```

---

## 模板 3：通讯作者（汇总请求 + 理由 + 确认全体同意）

```
Subject: Request: author list change for "[Manuscript Short Title]"

Dear Editor,

I am writing on behalf of all authors to request a change to the
author list of our manuscript entitled "[Full Manuscript Title]"
[ (MS# [number]) ].

Proposed change: [Added / Removed / Reordered] [Author Name]
[Specifics in one line]

Reason: [选一个：
  — 加人：[Name] contributed [具体贡献]，meeting authorship criteria
    per ICMJE recommendations.
  — 删人：[Name] was unable to participate in [the revisions /
    final review and approval] and has agreed to be removed.
  — 调序：The revised order reflects [Name]'s expanded role in
    [具体贡献 that changed between submission and revision].]

All authors (including those affected by this change) have agreed.
Individual consent emails are CC'd in this thread.

Updated author list:
[Author 1], [Author 2], [Author 3], ...

Sincerely,
[Corresponding Author Name]
[Affiliation]
[Email]

CC: [Author 1], [Author 2], [Author 3], ...
```

## 告诉用户——给 agent 的话术

你替用户逐份填好 `[brackets]` 之后：

> "我帮你写了三组邮件——未受影响作者、被影响作者、通讯作者各一份。名字和理由都按你的情况填好了。
>
> 接下来：
> 1. 把非通讯的邮件发给对应的人——让他们用自己的邮箱发出去，抄送通讯作者
> 2. 所有人发完之后，把汇总那封发给通讯作者，让他发——CC 所有人
> 3. 等着期刊回。可能要补 Authorship Change Form——看他怎么说再补
>
> 记住：发完这批同意邮件之后，别再碰通讯邮箱。以后任何联系编辑的事，让通讯去发。你（一作）发的编辑不认。"
